Just In Case Project

An e-commerce website with functioning e-catalogue, shopping cart, payment system, reviews section, personalisation capability and social media integration.   

Description

Just in case is an e-commerce site which I created with two of my friends. Our website is called Just in Case, because our idea was to sell personalised phone cases. On our e-commerce site customers can pick out the type of phone case that they want to buy and personalise it. 

You can visit the site @  http://justinphonecase.shop/Home.html
